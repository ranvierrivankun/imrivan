About

I am a graduate of the Informatics Engineering program in Software Engineering from Universitas Bina Sarana Informatika, with expertise in Web Development using the Laravel framework (Full Stack), PHP programming language, and MySQL database management.

Additionally, I possess skills in Linux operating system administration. I also have proficiency in network management, including the use of Mikrotik and switch management to ensure optimal network performance.

Furthermore, I have experience as an Electrical Technician, which includes room electrical installations, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems.

In the workplace, I am always committed to delivering the best according to my abilities. When facing failure, I take valuable lessons from those experiences and use them as motivation to continue growing and developing.
